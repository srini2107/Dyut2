About Us

At the core of our expertise lies the design and manufacture of high-performance composite propellers, engineered to deliver superior aerodynamic efficiency, reliability, and safety across all phases of flight. Each Dyut Systems & Technologies, propeller is crafted from premium-grade composite materials and undergoes rigorous validation for structural integrity and performance—resulting in lightweight, durable, and efficient solutions optimized for both unmanned and manned platforms.

Our Projects

Beyond propellers, Dyut Systems & Technologies provides end-to-end capabilities in propulsion systems, landing gears, fuel tanks, and customized composite structures, all tailored to specific mission and platform requirements. Our solutions combine advanced materials with precision manufacturing to ensure seamless integration, consistent quality, and long-term operational dependability. With a clear vision to empower the future of aerial mobility, Dyut Systems & Technologies partners with innovators, defense organizations, and OEMs to deliver reliable, high-performance composite solutions that redefine the possibilities of unmanned and experimental aviation.
